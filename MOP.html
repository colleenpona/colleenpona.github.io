Cooking Methods & Techniques

Sautéing

Quick cooking method using high heat and a small amount of fat. Perfect for vegetables, meats, and creating flavorful bases for sauces.

Braising

Slow cooking method combining both dry and moist heat. Ideal for tougher cuts of meat that become tender and flavorful.

Roasting

Dry heat cooking method in the oven. Creates beautiful caramelization and concentrated flavors in meats and vegetables.

Steaming

Gentle cooking method using moist heat. Preserves nutrients and natural flavors while creating light, healthy dishes.

Kitchen Tips

  • • Always read the entire recipe before starting to cook
  • • Mise en place - prepare and organize all ingredients first
  • • Taste as you go and adjust seasonings accordingly
  • • Keep your knives sharp for safer and more efficient cooking
  • • Don't overcrowd the pan when cooking - it lowers the temperature
